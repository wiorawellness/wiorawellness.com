# WIORA Wellness

WIORA Wellness is a premium aesthetic and wellness clinic located in
Andheri West, Mumbai, India.

The clinic provides medically supervised skin, hair, laser, and
nutrition treatments, with a focus on patient safety, ethical care,
and long-term results.

WIORA Wellness operates as a medical clinic and does not function as a
spa or beauty salon.
